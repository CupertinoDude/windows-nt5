Windows Product Key
Your Microsoft Windows Product Key is the 25-digit code printed on the front cover of your Windows XP Getting Started manual. The Product Key is printed above the barcode as shown in the example below.

Please locate your Windows XP Getting Started manual and type the Windows Product Key as it appears on the front cover.

- - - -
If you do not have your Product Key, click Skip.
To continue enter the 25-digit key and then click NEXT.
