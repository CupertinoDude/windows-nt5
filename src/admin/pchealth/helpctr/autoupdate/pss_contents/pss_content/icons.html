Icons on Desktop Not Arranged in Alphabetical Order

After you use "Auto Arrange" to arrange the icons on your desktop, you may notice that your "My Documents" icon comes before your "My Computer" icon on your desktop.

To work around this issue, remove the Auto Arrange check mark, and then arrange your icons manually. This behavior is by design.
