What's new in Windows

Now Microsoft Windows is more convenient and easier to use than ever.


Add new hardware easily
With Windows Plug and Play, you can plug new equipment into your computer and have it work instantly—without restarting!


Shut down safely and save energy too!
With the new Hibernate feature, you can shut down your computer safely, even while unsaved files are open. When you restart it you get everything back just as it was. Or you can use the Standby feature to make Windows instantly available (instead of having to restart), plus you save energy and reduce wear and tear on your computer.


Return your computer to an untroubled time
If your computer isn't working correctly, you can use Windows System Restore to make your computer work just like it did before the problem started.


Get fast solutions to problems automatically
When you have a problem with your computer, Windows Support Automation enables you to find it and fix it, fast.


Easily add accounts to a different computer
With Windows Internet Migration, you can easily duplicate your Internet Explorer and Outlook Express account information on another computer.


Read about other Windows benefits
Windows includes many new features. Your computer will start more quickly, and we've added protection for important system files. These improvements make Windows even more reliable.
