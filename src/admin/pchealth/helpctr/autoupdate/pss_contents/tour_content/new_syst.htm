System Restore

Sometimes the unexpected happens. You install a product or program—perhaps from a CD or the Internet—and suddenly your once reliable computer starts acting up. Somteimes even when you uninstall the program, your problems continue.

Now you can returns your computer to a state you specify, prior to when your problem started. Just use System Restore. You don't lose documents you've created or changes you've made to existing files. The only thing you lose is your problem. You get your computer's great performance back, and you avoid the frustration of having to go through a lengthy computer support call.
