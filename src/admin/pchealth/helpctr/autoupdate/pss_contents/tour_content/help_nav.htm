Navigating in Help and Support

There are many ways to find the information you need in Help and Support.

Contents:
Most people look for categories when they seek help. You can use the Contents list to do this. It appears on the left, organized into the categories that people usually search for help. Click an item in the list to see subcategories and find topics.

Search:
If you can't find what you're looking for in the Contents, you can search for a word or phrase. Use the Search bar near the top of the window by typing keywords in the box, such as printers, for example, and then clicking Go.

Index:
Or if you prefer, you can use the book-style Index by clicking Index at the top of the window.
