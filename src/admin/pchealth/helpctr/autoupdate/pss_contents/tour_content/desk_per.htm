Personalizing the desktop

One great feature about the desktop is that you can personalize it - from simply changing the color or adding a background pattern or photograph, to making your desktop come "alive" by adding dynamic content such as news headlines or stock quotes that are updated at regular intervals from the World Wide Web.

And personalization isn't just for fun: You can also add the programs you use most often to the Start menu and place shortcuts to your favorite programs and documents to the desktop so they're always just a click away.
