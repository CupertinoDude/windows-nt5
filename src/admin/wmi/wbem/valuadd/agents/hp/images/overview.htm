ManageX:
Focused, policy-based NT management

HP OpenView ManageX provides a powerful, easy-to-use management solution focused on the most critical needs of Windows NT® system administrators. This demonstration version includes only two policies, which send notifications based on NT Event Log events. But the complete ManageX product ships with more than 200 ready-to-run policies designed to ensure the availability and performance of mission-critical Windows NT® systems and BackOffice™ applications, especially in 24x7x365 environments. In addition, the ManageX Performance Monitor supplies real-time diagnosis of performance characteristics

Proactive management

ManageX is designed to maximize your NT investment not only by providing reactive notification when problems occur, but through proactive monitoring and reporting that can spot problems before they happen. In the full product, the Smart Reporter allows you to log system and application performance data for capacity planning, identifying trends, and troubleshooting. This information can be accessed through pre-packaged reports complete with information on possible causes and solutions.

ManageX also allows you to automate administrative tasks through application and event management. The built-in correlation engine provides exception-based management and customizable triggers for automated corrective actions.

The most advanced Windows NT® management solution

HP OpenView ManageX is 100% Windows NT® compatible and is based on the most advanced Microsoft technologies including Distributed Component Objects (DCOM), WMI, and ActiveX. ManageX is the industry's first solution based on the Microsoft Management Console (MMC) standard. If your organization depends on Windows NT®, ManageX works right now to provide the management solution for your environment.
