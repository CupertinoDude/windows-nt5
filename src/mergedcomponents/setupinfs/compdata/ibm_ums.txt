IBM Universal Management Services (UMS) is not compatible with this version of Windows. You must uninstall this service before continuing setup.

To uninstall IBM Universal Management Services: 

1. Click Start. 
2. Click Settings. 
3. Click Control Panel. 
4. Double-click Add/Remove Programs. 
5. Select IBM Netfinity Director.
6. Click Change/Remove.  
7. Click Yes to uninstall.
8. When the uninstall process has completed, click Yes to restart.

For more information about this utility, visit the manufacturer's Web site.
