Prepare settings for transfer

To prepare settings:
  1. Insert the Migration Wizard Program Disk into the disk drive of your original computer.

  2. Click Start, and then click Run.

  3. Type a:\migwiz and then click OK.

 After you have prepared the settings on your original computer, return to this screen and then click Next.


If you would like to skip copying files and settings for now, click Skip and you can run the Migration Wizard from the Start Menu after you finish setting up your computer.
